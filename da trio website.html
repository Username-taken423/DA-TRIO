What is Da Trio?

Da Trio is a friend group consisting of 3 members: Alfredo, Jeshaiah, and Marck Zian. It was formed in late 2024. Over its less-than-a-year history, Da Trio has had countless group chats, but only one has stood out as the alpha of all group chats: "Da Trio."

Initially, the group chat was meant for paranormal discussions. However, it evolved into a space for friends to connect. After a few weeks, it became the "4th Quarter RLS Group" for project collaboration. Once the project was completed, the group chat finally transformed into the "Da Trio" chat we all know and love today.
